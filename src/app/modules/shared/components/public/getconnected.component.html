Our Commitment to Students

We will connect you to:
  • Potential colleges, universities, and conservatories that offer a music degree
  • Potential institutions seeking your talents, even if you are not going to major in music
  • A college directory of U.S. colleges, universities, and conservatories, and the specific music degrees they offer
  • A national directory of collegiate premier programs
  • US Military music ensemble opportunities
  • A searchable profile, where you can upload your educational information, performance history, honors, awards, references, and performance videos
  • A national directory of non-institutional scholarships
  • A directory of music career opportunities
  • A directory of music camps, summer stock, and master classes
  • A directory of festivals and competitions
  • Resources about the application, audition, and acceptance process
  • A national repertoire directory of audition-appropriate pieces
  • Hundreds of schools’ music entrance exam questions and answers
  • 500 musical terms found in music entrance exams
  • A guide of general music history
  • The American Music Timeline

Our Commitment to Teachers

We will connect you to:
  • A platform where your students can upload their educational and performance history, references, and performance videos for collegiate and summer enrichment recruiters
  • A portal to upload and maintain a reference for each of your student’s needs.
  • A platform to follow your students’ collegiate process
  • A college directory of U.S. colleges, universities, and conservatories, and the specific music degrees they offer
  • A directory of non-institutional scholarships to aid your students, parents, and school counselors in the procurement of scholarship funds
  • A directory of music camps, summer stock, and master classes
  • A directory of festivals and competitions
  • A national repertoire directory of solos, appropriate for college auditions
  • Music department entrance exam questions and answers
  • A Resource Center of articles, guides, and musical exercises to facilitate your needs
  • A video series of Motivational Moments that can be used in your classroom
  • A support program (SOAR) for your underserved students

Our Commitment to Recruiters

We will connect you to:
  • A searchable database of prospective students, including their educational information, performance history, honors, references, and solo performance videos
  • A secure portal to review teacher references
  • A secure messaging portal for direct contact with students, parents, and teachers
  • A national directory of non-institutional scholarships
  • An opportunity for your school and music program to be featured on our front page
